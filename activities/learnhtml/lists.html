What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags are tags used to make text stand out or have added emphasis. Such examples include the (mark) tag that highlights text like this. Others include tags like the (strong) tag that boldens text for added emphasis.

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

UL stands for unordered list, and OL stands for ordered list.

What is a DL?

DL stands for description list. It is a way to group terms and their corresponding descriptions. It used to create definitions, glossaries or lists of items with details. The tag for DL is (dl) with its closing tag (/dl). Within the (dl) tags are the tags (dt) to define terms, and (dd) to describe said terms. The following is an example of a dl tag:

Samoa
A country situated in the Pacific ocean made up of 2 main islands: Upolu and Savaii
Cook Islands
A group of 15 islands located in the pacific ocean with said islands grouped as the northern and southern islands.
