Images

What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is best for photographs and images with complex details, smooth gradients, and no need for transparency, such as landscapes, portraits, and sunsets.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

You should encode photos or graphics with limited colors, sharp lines, and large areas of solid color using the GIF format.

What is unique about the PNG format?

Unlike JPEGs, PNGs support transparency with varying levels of opacity (alpha channel), and its ability to store a broad range of colors. Its uniqueness lies in its lossless compression.

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

You can use either a web-based resizing tool or software that is already installed on your device.

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

Photo editing software is generally preferred over using CSS for several key reasons, primarily related to performance, image quality, and bandwidth efficiency such as when loading a web page.
