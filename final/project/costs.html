costs

To help with those that are from the mainland of the US and/or others from other countries I have included the following table to help visualise the costs of owning a vehicle in the state of Hawaii.

Please note that this table only reflects the county of O'ahu in the state of Hawai'i. You will need to look up what the county laws are for the place you will be registering a vehicle. Also the total costs will be dependent on how heavy your vehicle is. Mahalo!

required costs in Hawai'i
fee type description cost applies to notes sources
State Registration Fee Required annual fee set by state of Hawai'i $46.00 Statewide Applies to all passenger vehicles source
Safety Inspection Mandatory yearly inspection for road safety complicance $25.75 Statewide Price increased in 2025 source
State Weight Tax Tax charged per pound of vehicle weight 1.75c - 2.25c per lb Statewide Dependent on total vehicle weight source
County Weight Tax Additional county portion charged per pound 7.0c per lb Honolulu County Minimum charge $12 source
Title/Ownership Transfer Fee when transferring ownership of a vehicle $5.00 Statewide Extra county processing fees may apply source
