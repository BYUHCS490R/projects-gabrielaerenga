about me

Aloha mai kakou! My name is Gabe and I am a current student at BYU-Hawaii. I made this web site to help other like-minded students here in Hawaii (or others) to encourage working on their own cars to save money and headaches when dealing with cunning mechanics and the beautiful but harsh weather conditions in Hawai'i.

Follow along with me to learn some great insights on how to look after your car in paradise on a student budget!
