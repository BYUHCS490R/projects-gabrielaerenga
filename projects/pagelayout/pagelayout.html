Finals Recap

In the closing rounds of the 2025 regular season, the Brisbane Broncos battled fiercely to secure a top-four spot. Their Round 27 clash with the Melbourne Storm was pivotal: Reece Walsh delivered a spectacular performance, scoring 2 tries, setting up others and executing a 40-20 to help Brisbane win 30-14 to seal 4th place on the ladder. The finish guaranteed them a "life" in the finals and qualifying final away in Canberra.

In Week 1, Brisbane faced the Canberra Raiders in a thrilling qualifying final at GIO Stadium. It turned ito a classic: after trailing later in the game, the Broncos fought back and in golden point extra time, Ben Hunt kicked the dramatic 39-metre field goal to win 29-28 and send Brisbane into the prelimiary final.

In the preliminary final, Brisbane took on the four-time defending premiers Penrith Panthers at Suncorp Stadium. Down 14-0 at halftime, the Broncos mounted a gutsy comeback in the second half. Kotoni Staggs struck first, Xavier Willison crossed, then Reece Walsh produced a precision offload to Deine Mariner in the corner to clinch a 16-14 victory.

In the Grand Final on October 5, 2025, Brisbane faced the Melbourne Storm at Accor Stadium. The Storm led 22-12 at halftime, but Brisbane flipped the script in the second half once again. Reece Walsh was immense: he scored a try, assisted 3 others, made vital try-saving tackles, and kept the Broncos in the fight after both halves - Adam Reynolds and Ben Hunt - went off injured. Brisbane eventually prevailed 26-22, breaking a 19-year premiership drought and claiming their 7th title.
