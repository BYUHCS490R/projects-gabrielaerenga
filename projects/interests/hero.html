My Hero

Interests Hero

Darren Lockyer

Darren Lockyer or otherwise known as Locky, is my favourite player of all time. He played for the Brisbane Broncos, Queensland Maroons and Australian Kangaroos. He debuted for the Brisbane Broncos in 1995 and retired in 2011.

He started out as a fullback in the game of Rugby League and then transitioned into the five-eighth position where he played for the rest of his career. He was fast, durable, skillful and a great tactician for the game. During his career he amassed a number of accolades, some which are included below:

Hall of Fame & General Awards

NRL Hall of Fame
Inducted in 2008
Sport Australia Hall of Fame
Inducted in 2018
Golden Boot Award
Won twice for international player of the year

Club & Representative Accolades

Dally M Awards
Won fullback of the Year multiple times and Five-eighth of the Year
Clive Churchill Medal
Awarded as best player of the 2000 Grand Final
Wally Lewis Medal
Awarded as best player of the series in the 2006 State of Origin
